Requirements Elicitation - List of additional requirements elicited:

1) Can a patient receive the same medication if they requested it once immediately before the previous day ended, and once immediately after the new day began? What timezone is "day" based on?

2) Does the system validate prescriptions and link them to the dispense event?

3) What uniquely identifies a patient, and must a patient have an ID to request the dispensing of a medication?

4) Can quantity dispensed exceed inventory, or must it be constrained?

5) Are there any rules to prevent early refills?

6) Does the system record time stamps, and how precise are they?

7) Does the system prevent dispensing medications with known interactions with the patient's currrent medications?

8) If dispensing is tied to a prescription, what fields are required?
